About Me

HELLO! My name is Sabrina Hollett and I am a full-stack web developer in Austin, Texas. I enjoy making dynamic and responsive websites for both mobile and desktop devices. My journey into coding stemmed from an interest in the process of building, creating, and often re-building websites. I'm detail-oriented and am always finding ways to make tasks more efficient. My previous work experience includes Event Team Management where I'd direct a team of volunteers for set up and breakdown of events of up to 2,000 people! My team members know I'm not afraid to jump in and get things done.



When I'm not working on a project build you can find me either on a dancefloor, in the wilderness, or maybe just sitting at home with my two adorable cats.
